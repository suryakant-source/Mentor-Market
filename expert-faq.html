Getting Started

The basics of joining MentorMarket as an expert.

Who qualifies as an expert on MentorMarket?

We look for professionals with 15+ years of experience in their domain. This typically means you've held senior leadership positions — Director level or above — at established organizations. We verify your employment history, credentials, and accomplishments through our review process.

You don't need a specific academic background or certifications beyond what's standard in your industry. What matters is that you've genuinely walked the path your potential mentees are trying to navigate.

We currently support 10 core domains: Finance, Legal, Operations, Healthcare, Technology, HR & Culture, Sales, Supply Chain, Marketing, and Real Estate. If your expertise falls outside these domains, we may still be able to accommodate you — reach out to our expert team.

How do I apply?

The application process takes about 15–20 minutes. You'll need:

  • Your current or most recent professional title and organization
  • A summary of your domain expertise (3–5 sentences)
  • Your preferred hourly rate
  • Availability (hours per week you can commit)
  • LinkedIn profile or CV for verification

After submitting, our team reviews your application within 48 business hours. If approved, you'll receive an email with next steps to complete your profile.

Is there a fee to join as an expert?

No — joining MentorMarket is completely free. There are no registration fees, no subscription costs, and no upfront charges of any kind. We only earn when you earn.

Our revenue comes from a platform fee taken on each successful booking. This is how we fund verification, platform maintenance, customer support, and video infrastructure — all the things that make your sessions possible.

What happens after I submit my application?

After submission, our team reviews your application within 48 business hours. We check:

  • Employment history and claimed titles
  • Educational credentials
  • LinkedIn verification
  • Reference checks (optional but strongly recommended)

If approved, you'll receive an email with a link to complete your expert profile — add your photo, bio, session policies, and set your calendar availability. Once your profile is live, buyers can find and book you immediately.

Why was my application rejected? Can I reapply?

Applications may be declined if we can't verify the credentials you provided, if your experience level doesn't meet our minimum threshold, or if your domain isn't currently supported on the platform.

Yes, you can reapply after 6 months. If your circumstances have changed (for example, you received a promotion or moved to a more senior role), your new application will be reviewed on its merits.

Earning and Rates

Everything about how experts earn on MentorMarket.

How do I set my hourly rate? What should I charge?

You set your own rate. We don't mandate or cap rates — they reflect the value you place on your time and expertise. Our experts charge anywhere from ₹2,000 to ₹10,000 per session, depending on their experience level and domain.

As a starting point, consider what you'd pay for an hour of high-quality professional advice in your field. If you're unsure, start modestly — you can raise your rate at any time, but increasing it after you've built a rating is harder.

How and when do I get paid?

Payments are processed on the 15th of each month for the previous month's completed sessions. We offer direct bank transfer (NEFT/RTGS) to Indian bank accounts at no cost. For international experts, we support PayPal and Wise transfers (transfer fees apply and vary by country).

Minimum payout threshold is ₹2,000. If your balance is below this, it carries forward to the next month.

Can I change my rate after my profile goes live?

Yes, you can update your rate at any time through your dashboard. Changes take effect for new bookings immediately — existing booked sessions are honored at the rate agreed upon at the time of booking.

Note: decreasing your rate won't affect your standing on the platform, but increasing it may prompt some buyers to reconsider. We recommend building a strong rating before raising rates significantly.

Can I offer free introductory sessions?

MentorMarket doesn't support free sessions on the platform. However, you can offer a discounted introductory rate (as low as ₹500) to let buyers try your style before committing at full price. Some experts use this approach very effectively.

The Session Experience

What to expect when you're mentoring on the platform.

What happens during a session? What is the format?

Sessions are conducted via video call through our integrated Zoom-based platform. You and the buyer join a dedicated session link that's generated when the booking is confirmed. Sessions typically run 45–50 minutes, with the last 5–10 minutes reserved for questions and next steps.

You have full control over session format. You can prepare an agenda, use screen sharing for presentations or documents, or simply have a free-flowing conversation. There's no script — you bring your expertise and experience.

What technology do I need?

A computer with a webcam and stable internet connection (10+ Mbps) is sufficient. For the best experience, we recommend:

  • A quiet, well-lit room for video calls
  • Headphones to avoid audio feedback
  • A second screen if you plan to share documents or slides
  • The Zoom desktop app installed (it's more stable than the browser version)

Are sessions confidential?

Expert Confidentiality Policy
  • What we collect: Session dates, expert and buyer names, billing information
  • What we share: Only session metadata with the expert and buyer involved
  • What we don't share: The content of your conversations, documents shared, or advice given
  • Session notes: Owned by the expert. You may take personal notes but not share or publish them without buyer consent
  • Disputes: Any confidential information dispute is handled privately and subject to our dispute resolution process

What if a buyer doesn't show up?

Buyers who miss sessions without notice are charged in full. You'll receive the full session fee regardless. For late cancellations (within 24 hours), the standard cancellation policy applies — the buyer forfeits their payment.

If a buyer is consistently not showing up, you can block them from booking future sessions through your dashboard.

Can I refuse a booking?

Yes. You can decline booking requests without explanation. We recommend declining politely and quickly so the buyer can find another expert. Frequent declines without engagement may affect your profile visibility in search.

Once you've accepted a booking and it appears on your calendar, cancelling is discouraged. If you must cancel, do so at least 48 hours in advance to avoid a late cancellation penalty.

Can I share documents or presentations during a session?

Absolutely. You can share your screen, upload files, or use third-party tools during sessions. Many experts prepare slide decks, financial models, or reference documents in advance. Some share a one-page summary after the session as a follow-up gift.

Note: MentorMarket doesn't store session recordings or shared screen content. Anything you share is ephemeral.

Platform Policies

The rules of the platform, explained clearly.

What is your cancellation and refund policy?

Buyers can cancel and receive a full refund up to 24 hours before the scheduled session. Within 24 hours, no refund is issued. If you cancel a confirmed booking more than 48 hours in advance, there's no penalty. Cancelling within 48 hours may affect your profile standing.

MentorMarket reserves the right to issue refunds in cases of demonstrated technical failure on our end.

How does the rating and review system work?

After each session, buyers are prompted to leave a 1–5 star rating and an optional written review. Your average rating is prominently displayed on your profile and affects how you appear in search results.

Reviews are published immediately. Experts can flag inappropriate reviews for our team to review. We remove reviews that violate our community guidelines (defamatory language, personal attacks, or content unrelated to the session).

Can I work with a buyer directly after meeting them on MentorMarket?

No. Our terms of service prohibit arranging sessions or any form of paid engagement outside the platform for 12 months after your first session with a buyer. This includes direct hiring, consulting engagements, or any other paid work — whether or not it relates to topics discussed in sessions.

Violating this policy results in immediate account termination. This policy protects both the platform and ensures you receive genuine mentorship rather than being used as a free consultant.

Is there an exclusivity requirement?

No. You can offer your expertise on any other platform or work with buyers independently — as long as those arrangements don't violate the non-circumvention policy above. MentorMarket is not an exclusive engagement.

What happens if I want to take a break?

You can pause your profile at any time. When paused, your profile is hidden from search results and no new bookings are accepted. Your existing bookings are honored, and your profile data and ratings are preserved.

To deactivate temporarily, go to Dashboard → Profile Settings → Availability → "Take a Break." There's no time limit — you can reactivate whenever you're ready.

How do I permanently delete my account?

You can permanently delete your account from Dashboard → Profile Settings → Delete Account. This action is irreversible — your profile, ratings, and payment history will be permanently removed.

Any pending bookings must be completed or cancelled before deletion. Final payouts are processed within 30 days of account deletion.
