Getting Started

Everything you need to hit the ground running.

Step 1: Complete Your Application

Fill out your profile with your experience, credentials, and availability. Our team will review within 3-5 business days.

Step 2: Get Verified

Our team verifies your credentials and employment history. You'll receive an email once you're approved.

Step 3: Set Your Schedule

Add your available time slots in the dashboard. You can update these at any time.

Step 4: Accept Your First Booking

Review incoming requests, accept one that matches your interests, and start mentoring!
