FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about MentorMarket.

How do I choose the right expert for my needs?
Browse our expert profiles by domain, read verified reviews, and check their experience. Each profile includes their background, areas of expertise, hourly rate, and ratings from other clients. You can also book a free 15-minute discovery call with some experts to see if they're the right fit before committing.
What happens if I need to cancel my session?
You can cancel or reschedule your session free of charge up to 24 hours before the scheduled time. Cancellations within 24 hours may be subject to a 50% fee. If the expert cancels, you'll receive a full refund automatically.
Are session recordings available?
Yes! All sessions are recorded and stored securely. You'll receive access to the recording within 24 hours after your session. Recordings are available for 30 days and can be downloaded for your personal use.
How are experts verified?
Every expert goes through a rigorous verification process. We verify their employment history, check references, validate their domain expertise, and review their professional accomplishments. Only experts who pass our comprehensive review receive the "Verified Expert" badge.
What payment methods are accepted?
We accept all major credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express), debit cards, and UPI payments. All transactions are processed securely through Razorpay. We don't store your payment details on our servers.
Can I become an expert on MentorMarket?
Absolutely! If you have 15+ years of experience in your field and want to share your knowledge, we welcome your application. Submit your profile through our expert registration form, and our team will review your application within 48 hours.
