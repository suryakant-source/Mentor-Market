About MentorMarket

Connecting the wisdom of retired industry leaders with the ambition of the next generation.

"

The most valuable lessons in business aren't taught in classrooms — they're earned through decades of building, failing, and building again. Our mission is to make that hard-won wisdom accessible to everyone.

— Raj Patel, Founder & CEO

Our Story

MentorMarket was born from a simple observation: the most impactful guidance often comes from those who've recently retired from the roles you aspire to. But accessing that wisdom was unnecessarily difficult.

We founded MentorMarket in 2024 to create a bridge between retired executives carrying decades of irreplaceable experience and professionals seeking that exact knowledge. Our platform makes it easy to find, book, and connect with verified experts across ten core business domains.

Today, we've facilitated over 2,400 sessions between verified experts and ambitious professionals. But we're just getting started.

Our Values

What We Believe

Three principles that guide everything we do.

Trust & Verification

Every expert on our platform is thoroughly vetted. We verify credentials, check references, and validate experience so you can book with confidence.

Respect for Time

Both experts and clients trust us with their time. We build tools that make every session count and every minute of guidance impactful.

Mutual Benefit

Experts earn meaningful income sharing their wisdom. Clients get career-defining guidance. Win-win is built into our model.
