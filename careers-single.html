Senior Frontend Engineer

Remote Full-time 5–7 years ₹25L – ₹40L

About This Role

As a Senior Frontend Engineer at MentorMarket, you'll own the interface that thousands of experts and buyers use every day to book sessions, share knowledge, and transform careers. This isn't a UI developer role — you'll be making architectural decisions, mentoring junior engineers, and shaping the product experience at a deep level.

In your first 90 days, you'll have shipped at least two major features, conducted your first code review for a junior engineer, and established yourself as a trusted voice in our technical discussions.

What You'll Do

  • Lead the development of new features in our React/TypeScript codebase
  • Architect and maintain our reusable component system used across all products
  • Collaborate with design and product to build polished, performant interfaces
  • Write clean, well-tested code (unit + integration tests, 80%+ coverage)
  • Mentor junior engineers through code reviews and pair programming sessions
  • Contribute to technical architecture decisions and documentation
  • Own end-to-end expert onboarding experience
  • Partner with founders to understand domain taxonomy

What We're Looking For

  • 5+ years of experience with React (hooks, context, performance optimization)
  • Strong proficiency in TypeScript and modern CSS (flexbox, grid, animations)
  • Experience with state management (Redux, Zustand, or similar)
  • Track record of building responsive, accessible web applications
  • Strong understanding of Core Web Vitals and web performance optimization
  • Experience with CI/CD pipelines and code quality tooling

Nice to Have

  • Experience with Next.js or Remix
  • Familiarity with video conferencing SDKs (Twilio, Daily, Zoom)
  • Experience building real-time collaborative features
  • Open-source contributions

Tech Stack & Tools

React TypeScript Next.js CSS Modules Redux Jest Playwright Figma Vercel

The Interview Process

1. Application Review
We read every application personally. You'll hear back within 3 business days.
2. Intro Call (30 min)
A casual conversation with our head of engineering to understand your background and motivation.
3. Take-Home Task
A real-world coding task that reflects the kind of work you'd do here. Takes 2–4 hours.
4. Technical Interview (60 min)
Live pair programming session. We'll build on the take-home task together.
5. Culture Fit (30 min)
Meet with two team members. We want to know you, not just your skills.
6. Offer
We make decisions quickly. Expect an offer within 48 hours of your final interview.
